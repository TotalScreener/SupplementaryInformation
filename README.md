# Supplementary Information

## Notice Regarding Availability of Supplementary Materials

This repository is intended to provide the **Supplementary Tables**, **Supplementary Figures**, and **Supplementary Information** associated with the following manuscript submitted to **MICCAI 2026**:

> **TotalScreener: Clinical Impact of a Trauma CT Decision Support System through Reverse-Engineering Diagnostic Process – An International Multicenter Model Development and Validation Study**

### Important Notice on Data Availability

The supplementary materials contained in this repository include information that has not yet been fully anonymized, such as the names of specific participating medical institutions and other identifying details related to the multicenter study.

In accordance with ethical and institutional disclosure policies, **these supplementary materials will be made publicly available upon official acceptance of the manuscript**.

We appreciate your understanding and patience. If you have any questions regarding the content of this repository, please contact the corresponding author.
